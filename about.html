About Me:

Born and raised in Central Texas, my journey into graphic design began in high school—like many creatives. I’ve always had a passion for making things. As a kid, I was constantly drawing, painting, experimenting with technology, sculpting with clay, building alongside my dad, playing drums and guitar, and capturing the world through a camera lens. I’ve always felt driven to express the beauty and feeling of what I saw around me.


That creative energy stayed with me into adulthood. After moving to Southern California, I rediscovered my love for graphic design, which quickly expanded into a passion for web development. I spent countless nights after work teaching myself everything I could—building websites day in and day out to sharpen my skills.


Today, there’s nothing more fulfilling to me than creating for the web—blending art and technology to bring ideas to life.
